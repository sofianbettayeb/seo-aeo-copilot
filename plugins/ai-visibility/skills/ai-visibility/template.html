How to read this report

The AI Visibility score is a 0–100 composite from the AEO Copilot platform. It blends three signals: how often the brand is mentioned across the tracked engines (60%), the technical readiness of the website for AI crawlers (25%), and the sentiment health of the mentions that do happen (15%). Grades follow standard A–F bands: A ≥ 85, B ≥ 70, C ≥ 55, D ≥ 40, F < 40.
